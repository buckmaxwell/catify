New here? We know it isn't pretty but this is it best free tool out there for creating genre based playlists in spotify.

To get started, you'll have to sync your spotify music.

This will sync your 'liked' tracks to your catify profile

Now it's time to edit your preferences

By default, we won't make ANY genre playlists, so this is important. Check your favorite genres to make playlists for.

Ready, set, go...

Time to click the magic button and get those playlists created. They will show up as something like "Catify's electronica" in your spotify.

Change your preferences as needed, and click this button whenver you like Whenever you add new songs to Spotify, you'll want to sync again.
